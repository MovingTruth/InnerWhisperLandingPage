Built for your eyes only.

InnerWhisper was designed from day one with the principle that your mental wellness data belongs to you, and only you.

  • No account required — ever. Open the app and start immediately.
  • No analytics, crash reporting, or tracking sent off your device.
  • No advertising networks or third-party SDKs of any kind.
  • Encryption keys secured in the iOS Keychain. Quote files encrypted with AES-256-GCM on disk.
  • Notification content is generated on-device — nothing sent to our servers.
  • Favorites and your added quotes sync via Apple’s iCloud infrastructure, not ours. Apple’s privacy policy applies.
  • Privacy manifest filed with Apple: tracking disabled, no personal data types declared.
